Our Services

What we make spotlessly clean for you

General Cleaning

Comprehensive and thorough cleaning for various locations, including homes, commercial buildings, factories, schools, and public spaces. We tackle everything that truly needs to be clean, with an eye for detail and hygiene.

Floor Maintenance

Professional maintenance and deep cleaning for all types of floors. Whether it's wood, PVC, marble, polished concrete, or natural stone, we ensure your floor regains its original shine and appearance.

Facade Cleaning

Give the exterior of your building a fresh look. We specialize in the sustainable cleaning of commercial and factory facades, treating all types of facade materials safely and effectively.

Glass & Solar Panels

Streak-free cleaning of interior and exterior glass, including washing windows of high-rise buildings. Additionally, we offer specialized cleaning of solar panels to keep your installation's efficiency optimal.

Special Cleaning

We provide discrete, respectful, and extremely thorough cleaning and disinfection after evictions and passing away. We ensure the location is delivered completely clean and fresh.

Industrial Cleaning

We have the expertise for specialized and heavy industrial ship cleaning, including the thorough cleaning of ship engines and engine rooms.

Final Construction Cleaning

A perfect final cleaning after a construction or renovation project. We remove all construction dust and dirt so that the building is immediately ready for handover to the customer or for direct use.
